ABOUT GLOSSARY

Glossary

🧍 Player Stats

  • PTS – Points scored by the player.
  • AST – Assists (passes leading directly to a made basket).
  • REB – Total rebounds (offensive + defensive).
  • OREB – Offensive rebounds (gaining possession after a missed shot).
  • DREB – Defensive rebounds (recovering the ball after an opponent’s missed shot).
  • STL – Steals (legally taking the ball away from an opponent).
  • BLK – Blocks (deflecting an opponent’s shot attempt).
  • TOV – Turnovers (losing possession without a shot attempt).
  • FG_PCT – Field Goal Percentage (FGM ÷ FGA).
  • FG3_PCT – Three-Point Field Goal Percentage (FG3M ÷ FG3A).
  • FG3M – Made 3-point field goals.

🏀 Team Stats

  • PTS_DIFF – Point differential (team points – opponent points).
  • AST_DIFF – Assist differential compared to opponent.
  • OREB_DIFF – Offensive rebound differential vs. opponent.
  • DREB_DIFF – Defensive rebound differential vs. opponent.
  • FG_PCT_DIFF – Difference in Field Goal % vs. opponent.
  • FG3_PCT_DIFF – Difference in 3PT % vs. opponent.
  • FG3M_DIFF – Difference in made 3PT shots vs. opponent.

📊 Outlier Definitions

  • Outlier – A game performance that deviates significantly from season average using a combination of percent difference and absolute difference thresholds.
  • Blended Scoring – A scoring model that weights both the percent increase and the raw increase in performance volume (e.g., scoring 36 vs. averaging 23).
